## Decisions Made

- The Node module is the sole schema, transition, boot-session, blocker-route, and classifier authority; the shell remains an imperative effect/IPC adapter.
- Strict classification is a separate crash-closed lifecycle: `not_run -> invoked -> completed`. A restart from persisted `invoked` blocks as invalid evidence without rerunning the classifier; persisted `classified` or `terminal` completes cleanup without rerunning either classifier or effect.

Persisted `authorized` or `invoked` state is ambiguous on restart and cannot be redispatched, while durable completion is normalized without redispatch.
